Pentatonic Scales

The foundation of guitar soloing

The Five Patterns

These five patterns of the minor pentatonic scale can be moved all over the fretboard. The key is determined by the root note (the red dot in these diagrams for A minor).

Pattern 1 (A Minor @ 5th fret)

E|---5---8---| B|---5---8---| G|---5---7---| D|---5---7---| A|---5---7---| E|---5---8---|

Pattern 2 (A Minor @ 8th fret)

E|---8--10---| B|---8--10---| G|---7---9---| D|---7---9---| A|---7--10---| E|---8--10---|

Pattern 3 (A Minor @ 10th fret)

E|--10--12---| B|--10--13---| G|---9--12---| D|---9--12---| A|--10--12---| E|--10--12---|

Pattern 4 (A Minor @ 12th fret)

E|--12--15---| B|--13--15---| G|--12--14---| D|--12--14---| A|--12--15---| E|--12--15---|

Pattern 5 (A Minor @ 15th or 3rd fret)

E|--15--17---| B|--15--17---| G|--14--17---| D|--14--17---| A|--15--17---| E|--15--17---|

ADD A SONG

Wrap chords in square brackets like [Am] right before the lyric they fall on. Saved in your browser only. Use Library → Export to back up.

YOUR LIBRARY

Your songs live only in this browser, on this device. Export regularly to back them up or to copy them to another device.

QUICK GUIDE

Add your first song

Tap + Add Song in the header. Paste your lyrics and put each chord in square brackets right before the syllable it lands on, like this:

[G]Heading [D]down south to the [Em]land of the [C]pines

Edit or delete

Open one of your own songs. The EDIT and DELETE buttons appear in the bottom controls. Sample songs are read-only; hide them all from the Library menu if you want a clean slate.

Back up your library

Open Library and tap Export to file. You'll get a JSON file you can save anywhere. To restore on the same or a different device, use Import & Merge.

Tools

The header tabs include a Tuner (uses your microphone), a Metronome, a pentatonic Scales reference, and a Practice tab with backing tracks, fretboard quiz, and ear training.

Where do my songs live?

In your browser's localStorage. They never leave your device unless you export them. If you clear your browser data, your songs are gone, so export occasionally.
